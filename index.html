About us

Learning support that meets students where they are.

InAmigos Foundation is a fictional education NGO created for this AI website generation task. The idea is simple: make practical digital learning accessible to students who need guided, local, and confidence-building support.

The website presents the organization as a bridge between schools, volunteers, and learners through skill labs, mentorship, career awareness sessions, and community events.

Services

Programs built for real progress

Each program is designed as a simple pathway from awareness to practice, portfolio building, and mentorship.

Digital Skill Labs

Hands-on sessions in computer basics, safe internet use, presentations, spreadsheets, and beginner coding.

Mentor Circles

Small groups guided by volunteers who help students set goals, ask questions, and practice communication.

Career Readiness

Resume basics, interview practice, portfolio projects, and workplace awareness for senior students.

Impact model

From classroom exposure to confident action.

The goal is not only to teach tools. It is to help learners feel capable of using technology to solve local problems, present ideas, and imagine better options for their future.

  1. 01

    Discover

    Students explore digital tools through short demonstrations and guided activities.

  2. 02

    Practice

    Learners complete projects in teams, receive feedback, and improve their work.

  3. 03

    Share

    Students present a final project to peers, mentors, and community members.
